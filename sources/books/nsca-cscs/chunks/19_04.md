Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance with feet

Upward movement:  On landing, immediately jump up as high as

these adjustme nts will change the height from which the exercise is

COMMON ERRORS

height
Not dorsiflexing the ankle in preparation for ground
contact; this negates the active flat-foot contact and
increases ground contact time
